## Operational risks checklist

- Data handoffs (duplication, mismatch, latency)
- Policy/process misalignment between teams/orgs
- Capacity constraints (queues, staffing)
- Exception handling (what happens off the happy path?)
- Auditing and traceability (for decisions)
